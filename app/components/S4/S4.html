Talk Things Out With Yourself

  • This technique involves letting yourself experience two opposite sides of a conflict or quandary, like taking both sides in a debate. Each voice gets the opportunity to discuss fully their side of the argument or their reason for making a particular choice, and the other gets to respond. This technique gives words to emotions, allowing feelings to be expressed and alternatives to be explored. You probably will feel silly at first, but give this exercise a fair chance. It really does work.
